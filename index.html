About me

I’m an applied scientist at Amazon Web Services (AWS), where I’m working on meeting summarization and theme detection in dyadic conversations.

I received my Ph.D. degree in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department from the University of Michigan in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Laura Balzano.
